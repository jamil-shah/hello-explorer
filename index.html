Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to support life. With a diverse range of ecosystems, from vast oceans to towering mountains, Earth provides a unique balance of atmosphere, water, and land that sustains millions of species. Its atmosphere, rich in oxygen and nitrogen, helps regulate temperature and shields life from harmful solar radiation. Earth's rotation on its axis creates day and night, while its orbit around the Sun governs the changing seasons. Home to humanity and a rich history of civilizations, Earth remains a dynamic and ever-evolving planet, teeming with natural beauty and scientific wonder.
